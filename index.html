About Isaac

I'm a somatic coach and Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu purple belt based in Singapore. My work sits at the intersection of body awareness, nervous system regulation, and the kind of honest self-inquiry that happens when you're under pressure on the mat.

After years of training, teaching, and sitting with people in hard conversations, I've come to believe that the body doesn't lie — and that real change begins when we stop treating it as an afterthought.

I work with individuals who are curious, honest, and ready to move beyond talking about change to actually feeling it.
